Why StatFest?

StatFest is a one day conference aimed at encouraging undergraduate students from historically underrepresented groups (African American, Hispanic, Native Americans) to consider careers and graduate studies in the statistical sciences. StatFest 2018 will be taking place on Saturday, September 22nd. Be sure to sign up today (see below)!

Visiting Pomona College

Pomona College is located in Claremont, CA, 35 miles east of downtown Los Angeles.

Parking

StatFest will take place in Millikan Laboratory at 610 N. College Ave., Claremont, CA 91711.

Street parking is available on College Avenue as well as the cross streets 6th Ave. and 7th Ave.

More information can be found at https://www.pomona.edu/visiting
